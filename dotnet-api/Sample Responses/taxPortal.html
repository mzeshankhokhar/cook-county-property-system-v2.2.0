Cook County Property Search

Cook County property owners are able to search for their records online. To begin, they may enter their 14-digit Property Index Number (PIN) or property address.

Billed Amounts & Tax History

Search to see a 5-year history of the original tax amounts billed for a PIN.  Once you search by PIN, you can pay your current bill online or learn additional ways to pay by clicking More Tax Bill Information on the next page.

The Cook County Treasurer’s Office provides payment status for current tax years and the ability to pay online.

Note: For prior tax years and status please check with the Cook County Clerk’s Office for more information. 

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Pay Online

Search for the amount of taxes billed and to make an online payment.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Property Description

Search for information about the property:

  • Property location
  • Township, tax rate and tax code
  • Property classification
  • Estimated property value and total assessed value
  • Building and lot size, where applicable
  • Tax bill mailing address associated with the property

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Tax Exemptions

Exemptions can reduce your tax bill. By performing the search you will see a 5-year history of the exemptions applied to your PIN.

If after you search by PIN and find you did not receive an exemption, click “More Exemption Information” under Exemptions on the next page to learn how to apply.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Refund Search

Search for potential refunds or overpayment associated with your property. This tool identifies duplicate tax payments or overpayments for which a refund may be obtainable. If you find a refund available, please follow the instructions under “More Refund Information” to apply.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Documents, Deeds & Liens

Search for a summary of the most recent recorded documents on your property. In addition, you will see recorded documents, deeds and liens available for purchase from the Cook County Clerk's website.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Tax Appeals, Dates & Deadlines

Search for information regarding the acceptance of Tax Appeals at the Assessor’s Office and at the Board of Review as well as if an appeal was filed with either office.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

Tax Rates

Tax rates are calculated by the Clerk’s Office based on the tax levies submitted by the county's taxing districts and the valuation of properties within those districts.

*If you do not know your PIN, use the "Search by Property Address" link.

More Record Information
All years referenced herein denote the applicable tax year (i.e., the year for which taxes were assessed). Parcels may from time to time be consolidated or subdivided. If information regarding a particular PIN appears to be missing for one or more tax years, it is possible that the PIN has changed due to a consolidation or subdivision. Users may contact the Cook County Clerk’s Office for information regarding PIN lineage. Users should also note that the information displayed on this site does not include special assessments (which are billed and collected by municipalities) or omitted taxes (which are assessed on an ad hoc basis by the Cook County Assessor’s Office). Please direct inquiries regarding the status of special assessments to your municipality. Questions regarding omitted taxes should be directed to the Assessor’s Office.
